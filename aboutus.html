Your Partner in Precision Flow Control

A premier industrial valve manufacturer in Mumbai, India, engineering excellence since 2001.

For over two decades, Usmani Tubes & Valves Co. Pvt. Ltd. (UTVC) has stood as a pillar of reliability in the Indian heavy engineering sector. Since our inception in 2001, we have evolved from a local supplier to a globally recognized manufacturer and exporter of industrial valves. We pride ourselves on delivering precision-engineered solutions that withstand the harshest industrial environments.

We are an ISO 9001:2015 certified company, compliant with international standards including API, ASME, ANSI, DIN, and BS. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Mumbai produces a versatile range of high-performance valves, including Gate, Globe, Ball, Check, and Butterfly Valves. These products are the lifeline of critical infrastructure projects across India, the Middle East, and Africa.

Serving Every Major Industry

Trusted by leaders in Petrochemicals, Power Generation, and Oil & Gas.

Our valves are designed to meet the rigorous demands of diverse sectors. We provide specialized fluid control solutions for the Oil & Gas (Upstream & Downstream), Thermal & Nuclear Power Plants, and Petrochemical Refineries. Our products ensure safety and efficiency in high-pressure and high-temperature applications.

Beyond energy, UTVC is a key partner for the Pharmaceutical, Water Treatment, Textile, and Chemical Processing industries. Whether handling corrosive chemicals, steam, or potable water, our valves—fabricated from Cast Iron, Cast Steel (WCB), Stainless Steel (SS304/SS316), and Forged Steel—guarantee zero-leakage performance and long service life.

Our Unwavering Commitment

Quality isn't just a standard; it's our manufacturing philosophy.

Our mission is to lead the industrial valve market by manufacturing superior-quality valves that enhance operational efficiency. We employ rigorous testing methodologies, including Hydrostatic Pressure Testing, Pneumatic Testing, and Material Analysis, to ensure every unit leaving our factory is flawless.

We believe in building long-term partnerships. By combining competitive pricing with strict adherence to delivery schedules, we provide exceptional value. Our team of skilled engineers works tirelessly to innovate, ensuring our IBR and Non-IBR valves meet the evolving needs of modern industry.

Engineering the Future

Aspiring to be the world's most trusted valve partner.

Our vision is to expand our global footprint while maintaining our commitment to sustainable manufacturing practices. We aim to set the industry benchmark for integrity and innovation. As we invest in advanced CNC machining and automation, UTVC remains dedicated to being the forefront solution for Industrial Fluid Control globally.
